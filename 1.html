903 bus stop route

Route introduction

Welcome to the University of Nottingham free hopper bus.903 bus runs mainly between the Jubilee Campus and University Park .



Ride mode

The hopper buses use a 'tap-on' system, so if you have your student card with you, please tap the card reader machine next to the driver. If you don't have a card, the driver will manually record your journey.
